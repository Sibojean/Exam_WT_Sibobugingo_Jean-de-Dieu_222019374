OUR SERVICES

Our platform offers a comprehensive range of virtual occupational therapy services designed to meet the needs of various individuals. Here are some of the key services we provide:

  • Teletherapy Sessions: We offer individual and group therapy sessions conducted via secure video conferencing platforms, allowing clients to receive tailored interventions from the comfort of their own homes.
  • Assessments and Evaluations: Our therapists conduct comprehensive assessments to understand clients' needs and create personalized treatment plans. These assessments are crucial for setting goals and tracking progress.
